#                                 Petition Engine  - Campaign Functions                                 #
#                                                                                                       #

Please see mainreq.txt firstly.

1-) Anyone can start a campaign.

2-) Core members (who are elected) of a organization can start campaigns on
 behalf of related organization

3-) Members of organization can veto the campaign started by a core member of related organization

4-) Anyone can join a campaign as petitioner

5-) Any petitioner can subscribe to a campaign's posts

6-) Campaigns may have tags to show similarities with campaigns
